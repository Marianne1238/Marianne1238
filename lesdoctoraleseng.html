Les doctorales


About us

We warmly invite doctoral students and young PhDs who are interested in geography and its epistemology to respond to the call for papers for the first colloquium of the International Network of Geography Doctoral Students, JI GEO - Junior International Geographers - supported by the French National Geography Committee (CNFG).

This network aims to promote meetings and collaborations between young researchers in geography around the world. The objectives of the panels are therefore to stimulate exchanges around the production processes of research in geography and to participate in the enrichment and scientific animation of a transversal and international network.

This colloquium entitled "Epistemology, methodology and reflexivity in geography: diversifying and renewing views to embrace practices" will take place in Le Mans, France, with physical presence* and remote participation formats, from 16 to 18 November 2022

We make the assumption that the current form of scientific meetings tends to standardize the content by formatting a way of producing thought (10-15 minutes of communication, presentation format, etc.). This is why these panels will allow longer and freer interventions (video, dialogue, gesticulated conference, participative theatre, sound immersion, etc.) and will favour times of exchange beyond simple questions and answers.

You will find the full texts of the CfP in download.

The modalities for responding to this call for papers are as follows :

- Responses due by 15 June 2022 to be sent to j.i.geographers@gmail.com

- This proposal should be a maximum of 3,000 characters including spaces,

- Collaborations between fields of geography, as well as interdisciplinary, inter-university, and international collaborations, or between research groups, will be privileged (Do not hesitate to ask for the directory to facilitate your response, to subscribe to the directory, click here: https://lnkd.in/gqrAGz6H),

- The full text of the papers will be expected by the beginning of October 2022: 25,000 characters (including spaces). If necessary, other ways of formalizing the full proposal will be considered (video, audio, etc.).

* The organizing committee will help you to finance your travel.
